alumni story

Welcome to the University of Information Technology and Sciences (UITS) Alumni Association website.
Since the inception of the UITS in the year 2003, it has produced a significant number of graduates, many of whom are well established, experienced and eager to set a link with their Alma mater and do something good for the University to flourish it as a center of excellence in the academic arena of the world. The university also needs the contribution of its magnificent Alumni to cherish its goal. To ease the reciprocal procedure, the University has embarked on a plan to introduce in its administrative organogram the ALUMNI sector in an attempt to establish contacts with the old students, home and abroad, and to benefit from their counseling, guidance and help in every respect for the all-round development of their Alma Mater.
The UITS Alumni Association (UAA) engages alumni in supporting the University. We provide exceptional value and ongoing support to our alumni by offering a sense of belonging, community, connection, and pride. To actively engage and support current and future alumni in a life-long relationship with UITS.
